## Mini gerador de links UTM (manual)

Base:

```text
https://www.vardasorte.com/oferta/?utm_source=<origem>&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=offer_post&utm_content=<formato>
```

Regras:
- `utm_source`: `telegram` ou `instagram`
- `utm_medium`: manter `social`
- `utm_campaign`: manter `offer_post`
- `utm_content`: muda conforme o formato/post

Sugestoes de `utm_content`:
- `canal` (post no canal Telegram)
- `grupo` (grupo/broadcast Telegram)
- `bio` (link da bio Instagram)
- `story` (Instagram Story)
- `reel` (descricao de Reel)
- `post_feed` (post de feed)

Exemplo rapido (Reel Instagram):

```text
https://www.vardasorte.com/oferta/?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=offer_post&utm_content=reel
```
